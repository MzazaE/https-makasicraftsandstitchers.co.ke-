      Makasi Crafts and Stitchers – Home of Stitchery

      Located in the vibrant heart of Nairobi, Kenya, Makasi Crafts and Stitchers is a trusted name in high-quality stitching and custom apparel solutions.
      With a reputation for precision, style, and unmatched craftsmanship, we specialize in creating a wide range of tailored garments and accessories. Our offerings include premium graduation attire, durable school uniforms, professional company wear, unique bags, stylish shoes, and general stitching services to meet every need. At Makasi Crafts and Stitchers, our mission is to bring each client’s vision to life with exceptional attention to detail and a commitment to quality. Whether outfitting students, professionals, or organizations, we ensure that each piece reflects the finest in fabric selection, stitch quality, and finish. Our skilled team of artisans and designers leverage years of experience and passion for the art of stitchery to deliver products that stand out.
